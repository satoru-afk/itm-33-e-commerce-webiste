Our Story

Growing Technology, Naturally

Harvest Tech began in 2020 when three friends - Sam, Lily, and Theo - left their jobs at big tech companies to pursue a simpler approach to technology. Having grown up in rural communities, they shared fond memories of peaceful farm life and believed technology should bring the same sense of calm and purpose.

Our laptops are designed with this philosophy in mind: technology that works as reliably as the seasons, providing tools that enhance your life without overwhelming it. Each product is crafted to be intuitive, durable, and environmentally conscious ‚Äì just like a well-tended farm.

What started as a small workshop in Sam's family barn has grown into a beloved brand, but we've kept our roots. We still test every laptop in our countryside headquarters, making sure each one delivers the peaceful, reliable experience our customers have come to expect.
